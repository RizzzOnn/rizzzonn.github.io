About Me

A visual storyteller, a student and a Aspiring Frontend Developer | Senior Data Entry & Education Analyst

Hi there, I am Rijan Manandhar , a visual storyteller and aspiring frontend developer. My work explores urban minimalism and the quiet emotions hidden within everyday spaces. Through each frame, I seek to create stillness, reflection, and a subtle connection - revealing beauty in moments that often go unnoticed.

I look for calm within chaos and meaning within the ordinary. Shadows, textures, and fleeting light become elements of expression, shaping a mood rather than a message. What is captured is not just a scene, but a feeling - something gentle, lingering, and quietly alive.
